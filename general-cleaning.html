Regular House Cleaning Services in Western New York

Queen City Cleaning provides professional weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly house cleaning services throughout Buffalo, Rochester, Syracuse, and all of Western New York. Our regular maintenance cleaning includes everything you would do yourself - dusting, sweeping, mopping, vacuuming, and polishing - but with professional efficiency and attention to detail.

Every new client starts with a comprehensive Deep Clean to establish the perfect foundation. This ensures we reach all those hard-to-access areas and remove any stubborn buildup, making subsequent maintenance cleanings more effective and keeping your home consistently beautiful.
